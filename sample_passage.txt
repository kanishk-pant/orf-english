The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This sentence contains every letter of the alphabet and is commonly used for typing practice. Reading fluency is an important skill that helps students comprehend text more effectively. When students read fluently, they can focus on understanding the meaning rather than struggling with individual words. This improves their overall reading comprehension and makes reading a more enjoyable experience. Teachers use various methods to assess reading fluency, including timed readings and oral assessments. The goal is to help students become confident, expressive readers who can read with appropriate speed, accuracy, and expression.
